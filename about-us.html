About Us

At Rent J E R, we believe that every event deserves to be extraordinary. Based in Lubbock, Texas, we are proud to offer top-quality party rental services designed to bring joy, excitement, and convenience to our community. Whether you’re planning a small family gathering or a grand celebration, our mission is to provide the tools and support you need to make it an unforgettable experience.

Our extensive inventory includes a variety of party essentials such as bounce houses, water slides, tents, tables, chairs, and more. We understand that every event is unique, which is why we offer customizable solutions to meet your specific needs. From lively birthday parties and school functions to elegant weddings and corporate events, Rent JER has everything you need to make your vision a reality.

What sets us apart is our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team goes above and beyond to ensure your event planning process is stress-free and enjoyable. From the initial consultation to the final setup, we provide professional, reliable, and timely service to exceed your expectations.

As a locally owned and operated business, we take pride in serving Lubbock and the surrounding areas with integrity and dedication. We prioritize safety and cleanliness, ensuring all our rentals are meticulously maintained and ready for your event. Our affordable pricing options make it easy to create memorable experiences without compromising on quality.

At Rent J E R, we don’t just provide party rentals—we help you create lasting memories. Explore our website or give us a call to discover how we can make your next event truly special. Let us be part of your celebration and bring your ideas to life with ease and excitement.
